Project story

The analysis was designed to answer one question clearly: where did RCB create or lose match control across batting phases, bowling phases, and individual player impact?

Analytical goal

Turn raw cricket score data into a decision-ready view.

The workbook converts innings scorecards and ball-by-ball tables into ranked batting performance, bowling impact, phase summaries, opponent comparisons, and searchable dashboard sections.
